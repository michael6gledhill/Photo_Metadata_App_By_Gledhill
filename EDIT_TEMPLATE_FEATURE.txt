TEMPLATES:

NAMING CONVENTIONS:
1. Select a naming convention from the list
2. Click "Edit" to modify it
   - Convention name is read-only
   - Pattern can be changed
   - Click "Save Convention" to apply changes
3. Click "Remove" to delete it
